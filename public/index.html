Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my student status?

Simply sign up with your .edu email address. We automatically verify your student status through our secure verification system. If you don't have a .edu email, you can upload your student ID or enrollment verification.

Is this portal completely free?

Yes! This portal is 100% free for all verified students. We believe education resources should be accessible to everyone. We're supported by partnerships with universities and optional premium features for institutions.

How do you ensure transaction safety?

We use secure payment processing, verified student profiles, and encourage meetups in safe, public campus locations. Our rating system and community guidelines help maintain a trustworthy environment.

Can I use this portal at any university?

We're rapidly expanding to universities nationwide! Currently available at 50+ institutions with new campuses added monthly. Contact us if your school isn't listed yetâ€”we prioritize based on student demand.
